Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish made of flat strips of pasta with melted cheese on top and red sauce with meat between each layer.

Pasta can be altered heavily to suit the tast of the chef but that is the most classic version. It is traditionally made in a pan and cut into squares to be served.

It keeps exceptionally well as a leftover as well.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Red Sauce with meat
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Cut pasta
  2. Smear sauce
  3. Apply herbs and spices to sauce
  4. Add the next pasta layer and repeat
  5. Add cheese to top layer
  6. Bake until cheese is browning on top
