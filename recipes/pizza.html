Lasagna

Description

Pizza is an all time classice food loved around the world.

Pizza can be altered wildly dependent on the preferences of the chef with limitless toppings options.

It keeps exceptionally well as a leftover as well.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Red Sauce
  • Dough
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Roll dough into a thin circle
  2. Smear sauce
  3. Apply cheese all over
  4. Add toppings
  5. Cook until the cheese browns
